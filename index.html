Site factors follow the hemispherical-photography convention (0 = fully obstructed, 1 = open ground). Energy uses a simple clear-sky model (Kasten–Young air mass, DNI = 1361 × 0.7AM0.678, diffuse = 10% of DNI) — an estimate, not a calibrated measurement.
